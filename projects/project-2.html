Case Study

Fitness Tracking Mobile App

A cross-platform mobile app that tracks workouts, nutrition, and wellness using AI-driven goals and personalized routines.

Project Overview

Built a mobile fitness companion app focused on seamless tracking, intelligent coaching, and actionable insights to help users meet their health goals.

Category: Mobile Development

Status: Completed

Date: December 20, 2023

Challenge

Users needed a unified experience for tracking exercise, meal plans, and wellness metrics while staying motivated over time.

Solution

Created a smooth mobile experience with custom workout plans, AI nutrition guidance, and reminder systems for habit formation.

Impact

Increased user retention by 42% and helped users improve activity consistency by 60% within the first six weeks.

Technologies Used

React Native Firebase Machine Learning

Features Delivered

  • Smart workout scheduling and progress tracking
  • Automated nutrition logging and meal recommendations
  • Personalized AI-driven coaching and reminders
  • Integrated wellness analytics and habit dashboards
